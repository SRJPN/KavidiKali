KAVIDIKALI GAME
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Architecture:-
    • Board Structure:-
      • 5 by 5 board
      • 4 sides
      • 25 tiles including one home at the center of the board
      • board has 2 loops - outer loop and inner loop
      • 4 safe places in the outer loop at a distance of 3 tiles
      • Each side has one safe place of outer loop
    • Players:-
      • 4 players
    • Coins:-
      • each player has 4 Coins of same colour
      • each player is differentiated by colours
    • Dice:-
      • has 6 sides with number from 1 to 6

  2. Constraints:-
    • Each side of the board is the entry point of each player
    • Each player has 4 coins to play with. His/Her target is to take all the four coins from the starting position outside the board to the main home.
    • The coin can enter only through safe place.
    • The player has to get 6 on dice while dicing to enter each Coin.
    • If the player gets 6 on dice while dicing he/she will get extra chance.
    • While dicing what the player gets on dice the player will move coin according to that number
    • After making a throw, if a player has valid moves, he/she will make the moves, if not, the next player gets his/her chance to make the throw in an anti clockwise order.
    • After finishing of valid move of current player next player gets chance.
    • The player want to round the outer loop atleast one time.
    • If the move has resulted in killing a coin(replace a coin )of opponents, current player gets an extra chance.
      He/she is also given permission to enter inner loop . Otherwise he/she can not enter into inner loop but he/she can possible to round on outer loop repeatedly.
    • The player can not kill the opponent's coin that are in any of the safe places.
    • Multiple pieces of the same player or opponents can be placed together in a safe place.
    • The killed coin has to get out from board.This coin can enter to the game by getting 6 on dice and should repeat the same path.
    • The coin has to round inner loop to get into the home.
    • When all coins of a player gets into the main home he/she will win the game. Then the game finished.
